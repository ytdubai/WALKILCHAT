## Where to Register (Priority Order)

### Priority 1: UAE (Your Base) 🇦🇪

**Authority:** Ministry of Economy - Trademark Department

**Process:**
1. Search existing trademarks (online)
2. Prepare application documents
3. Submit online or in-person
4. Pay fees
5. Wait for examination (2-3 months)
6. Publication period (30 days)
7. Registration granted

**Cost:** ~AED 3,000-5,000 ($800-$1,400)

**Documents Needed:**
- Trade license copy
- Passport copy
- Logo (high-resolution)
- Power of attorney (if using agent)

**Timeline:** 4-6 months

**Online:** https://eservices.economy.ae

### Priority 2: Nigeria 🇳🇬 (Biggest Market)

**Authority:** Commercial Law Department, Federal Ministry of Industry

**Process:**
1. Trademark search
2. File application
3. Examination
4. Publication in Trademark Journal
5. Registration

**Cost:** ₦50,000-100,000 ($110-220)

**Timeline:** 12-18 months

**Contact:**
- Commercial Law Department
- Abuja, Nigeria
- Online: https://iponigeria.com

### Priority 3: Kenya 🇰🇪

**Authority:** Kenya Industrial Property Institute (KIPI)

**Cost:** KES 5,000-10,000 ($40-80)
**Timeline:** 18-24 months

### Priority 4: International (Madrid Protocol)

**WIPO (World Intellectual Property Organization)**

**Covers:** 130+ countries with one application

**Cost:** $1,000-2,000
**Timeline:** 12-18 months

**Benefits:**
- One application, multiple countries
- Cost-effective for 3+ countries
- Centralized management
