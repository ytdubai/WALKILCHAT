## Partnership Agreement Template

### Standard Country Partner Agreement

**Term:** 3 years, renewable

**Territory:** Exclusive rights to [Country/City]

**Investment Required:**
- Warehouse space: 500+ sqm
- Staff: Minimum 5 people
- Working capital: ₦2M
- Quality control equipment: ₦500k

**Revenue Breakdown:**
- Transaction fees: 2% (partner keeps)
- Fulfillment fees: ₦200/item
- Storage fees: ₦50/item/month
- Premium placement: 50% split

**Performance Requirements:**
- Quality rejection rate: <5%
- Delivery time: <48 hours within city
- Customer satisfaction: >4.5/5
- Monthly volume targets

**WakilChat Provides:**
- Platform access
- Technology & updates
- National marketing (₦1M+ budget)
- Training programs
- CRM & analytics tools
- Payment processing
- Customer support infrastructure

**Exit Terms:**
- Either party: 90 days notice
- Partner can sell territory rights (with WakilChat approval)
- WakilChat has right of first refusal

## Financial Model - Partner vs Own

### Own Everything (Traditional):

**Capital Needed Year 1:** ₦50M ($110k)
- Warehouses: ₦20M
- Staff: ₦18M
- Equipment: ₦5M
- Operations: ₦7M

**Profit Margin:** 5% (all fees to WakilChat)
**Break-even:** Month 18-24

### Partner Model:

**Capital Needed Year 1:** ₦5M ($11k)
- Platform development: ₦3M
- Marketing: ₦1M
- Operations: ₦1M

**Profit Margin:** 3% (share 2% with partners)
**Break-even:** Month 6-9

**BUT:**
- 10x faster launch
- Lower risk
- Easier to scale
- Partners handle operations

## Recommended Approach

### HYBRID MODEL (Best of Both)

**Start: 100% Partnerships (Year 1)**
- Zero capital expenditure
- Fast launch
- Test markets
- Prove model

**Scale: Strategic Ownership (Year 2)**
- Buy out top-performing partners
- Own Lagos hub (biggest market)
- Keep partners in smaller markets

**Mature: Mixed Portfolio (Year 3+)**
- Own: 3-5 key hubs
- Partners: 20-30 smaller markets
- Balance control vs capital efficiency
