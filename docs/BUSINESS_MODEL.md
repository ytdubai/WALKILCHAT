## Part 3: Payment Escrow System

### How Money Flows

**When Customer Orders:**
```
Customer pays ₦10,000
↓
Money goes to WakilChat ESCROW account
↓
Status: "Payment Held"
```

**When Seller Ships:**
```
Seller ships to warehouse
↓
We receive & inspect
↓
Status: "Item Verified"
```

**When Customer Receives:**
```
Customer confirms delivery
↓
7-day satisfaction period
↓
If happy: Money released to seller (₦9,500)
If unhappy: Refund to customer (₦10,000)
```

**WakilChat Keeps:**
- 5% transaction fee (₦500)
- This covers: warehousing, shipping, insurance, operations

### Payment Timeline

| Action | Timeframe | Money Location |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| Customer orders | Day 0 | WakilChat escrow |
| Seller ships | Day 1-2 | WakilChat escrow |
| We receive | Day 3 | WakilChat escrow |
| We verify | Day 3 | WakilChat escrow |
| We ship to customer | Day 4 | WakilChat escrow |
| Customer receives | Day 5 | WakilChat escrow |
| 7-day satisfaction | Day 5-12 | WakilChat escrow |
| Seller gets paid | Day 12 | Seller account |

**Seller Gets Paid:** 7-12 days after shipping (industry standard)

## Part 10: Escrow & Payment System Details

### The WakilChat Guarantee

**Customer Protection:**
1. Pay for item
2. Money held in escrow
3. Item must arrive within 7 days
4. Must match description
5. 7-day return window
6. If anything's wrong → FULL REFUND

**Seller Protection:**
1. Ship item with tracking
2. We verify it's legit
3. Guaranteed payment (no chargebacks)
4. Protected from fraud customers

### The Escrow Account

**Structure:**
- Separate bank account (not operational funds)
- Audited monthly
- Insurance coverage
- Legal trust structure

**Example:**
```
Customer pays: ₦10,000
WakilChat holds: ₦10,000
WakilChat fee: ₦500 (5%)
Shipping: ₦800
Seller receives: ₦8,700

Timeline:
- Day 0: Customer pays → escrow
- Day 3: Item arrives at warehouse
- Day 5: Shipped to customer
- Day 6: Customer receives
- Day 13: Seller gets paid (7 day buffer)
```

### Fraud Prevention

**Red Flags:**
- New seller, expensive item → manual review
- Multiple failed deliveries → seller banned
- Customer disputes 50%+ orders → buyer banned
- Unusual payment patterns → AI flags

**Verification:**
- Sellers provide ID
- Phone verification
- Business registration (optional)
- Social media profiles
- Previous platform history
