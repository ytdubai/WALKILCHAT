## Option 2: Nigerian Trademark (CHEAPEST - $50-110)

### Nigeria Industrial Property Office

**DIY Cost:**
- Application fee: ₦10,000 ($22)
- Search fee: ₦5,000 ($11)
- Registration fee: ₦20,000 ($44)
- Publication fee: ₦15,000 ($33)
- **Total: ₦50,000 ($110)**

**Process:**
1. Online search: https://iponigeria.com
2. Download forms
3. Fill application
4. Pay at bank
5. Submit to Lagos office
6. Wait 12-18 months

**Protection:** Nigeria only (but your biggest market!)

## Option 3: Kenya Trademark (SUPER CHEAP - $40)

### Kenya Industrial Property Institute (KIPI)

**Cost:** KES 5,000 ($40!)

**Process:**

2. Create account
3. File electronically
4. Pay via M-Pesa
5. Done!

**Timeline:** 18 months
